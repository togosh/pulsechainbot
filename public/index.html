PulseChain Support, Simplified.

This is an AI-powered chatbot designed to answer your questions about the PulseChain ecosystem. It reads official documentation to give you accurate, helpful answers. Try asking it something below!

Have Feedback or Ideas? Let Me Know!

What The Bot Knows

The chatbot's knowledge comes directly from official and community-vetted sources. It can help you with:

Wallet Setup

Guides on installing MetaMask, Internet Money, or Brave Wallet and adding the PulseChain network.

Buying & On-ramping

Instructions for buying PLS with a bank using services like 0xCoast or bridging from other chains.

Bridging Assets

Details on using the PulseChain Bridge and other bridges to move assets from Ethereum, Base, and more.

Trading on PulseX

How to use PulseX to swap tokens on PulseChain.

HEX & Staking

Information on how HEX works, staking mechanisms, and common questions.

Developer Info

Details for developers, including contract addresses, RPC info, and how to run a node.
